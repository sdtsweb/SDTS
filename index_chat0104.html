Our Mission

San Diego Tamil Sangam (SDTS) is dedicated to uniting and enriching the Tamil community in the Greater San Diego region by fostering the growth and preservation of Tamil culture, language, and heritage. We provide a vibrant platform for cultural exchange, education, and celebration.

Introduce children to Tamil language and culture in a fun and engaging environment. Classes are held on Fridays at Shoal Creek Elementary School.
