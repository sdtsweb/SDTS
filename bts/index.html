About Tamil Language

தமிழ் தொன்று தொட்டு இருக்கின்ற மொழிகளில் ஒன்று ஆகும். இதன் வரலாறு சுமார் 2000 BCல் இருந்தே உள்ளது. தமிழ் மொழி சங்க காலத்தில் மிகவும் செழுமை அடைந்தது.

Tamil is one of the ancient languages in the world dating back to 2000 BC. Tamil language grew on the basis of three Tamil Sangam periods. Sangam period gave Tolkaapiyam ("Ancient Literature"), Ettuthogai ('The Eight Anthologies'), Pathupaatu ("Ten Songs"), Thirukural ("Sacred Verses"), Silapatikaram, Seevaga Sinthamani, Manimekalai and later Periya puranam, Kamba Ramayanam, Bharathiyaar and Bharathi Dasan songs gave rich literature in Tamil.

Tamil is one of the most prominent ancient language still spoken, written and is being actively developed. Tamil is an official language in India, Singapore and Malaysia. Learning Tamil and practicing Tamil culture will enhance our life and spirit.

School Mission

நம் பள்ளியின் நோக்கம், மூன்று வயதிற்கு மேல் உள்ள குழந்தைகளுக்கு தமிழ் மொழி, பண்பாடு ஆகியவற்றை அறிமுகம் செய்வதாகும்.

At Bharathiyar Tamil School, our goal is to introduce children from the age of three years and up, to Tamil language and culture. Classes are taught in a fun, engaging, stress-free environment, with emphasis on conversation skills, reading and writing. The school follows the comprehensive, structured syllabus developed by the American Tamil Academy.

The school also creates opportunities for children to participate in cultural activities such as plays, songs and dance performances, display their budding skills on the stage, and interact with speakers who are thought leaders in the community and learn from them.

School Administration

How We Operate

The school is a part of the San Diego Tamil Sangam, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization registered in the State of California.

The school is run by an Advisory Committee comprising members of the SDTS Executive Committee as well as school staff, to ensure all decision making is democratic and fair.

The advisory committee works closely with the teachers and other volunteers to:

  • Schedule classes, tests, parent-teacher conferences, annual programs, other events, and practice sessions for cultural performances.
  • Find alternate locations when the local school site is not available.
  • Recruit new teachers when needed.
  • Bring in community leaders to interact with the students.
  • Handle other administrative aspects.

National Tamil Spelling Bee

தமிழ்த் தேனீ என்பது அமெரிக்க அளவில் நடைபெறும் தமிழ் மொழிப் போட்டி ஆகும். இது மாணவர்களின் தமிழ் பயணத்தை ஊக்குவித்து, அடுத்த தலைமுறைக்குத் தமிழை எடுத்துச் செல்லும் முயற்சியை பாராட்டுகிறது.

National Tamil Spelling Bee is a US-wide contest that promotes Tamil among children, honors their effort, and helps pass the language to the next generation.

Students from registered Tamil schools can compete at the State, Zonal, and National levels. Levels are based on age and skill. FeTNA hosts the contest, with final rounds held during its annual conference. Our school participates every year.

BTS Thirukkural Competition

இது குழந்தைகள் திருக்குறளில் உள்ள மதிப்புமிக்க கருத்துக்களை தெரிந்து கொள்ளவும் அவர்களின் நினைவாற்றல், சிந்தனைத்திறன், நல்லெண்ணங்கள், தன்னம்பிக்கை மற்றும் படைப்பாற்றலை மேம்படுத்தும் வடிவங்களில் திருக்குறளை கற்றுக்கொள்ள உதவுகிறது.

BTS organizes this annual competition to help children connect with the timeless values of Thirukkural through creative formats that enhance memory, thinking skills, moral values, confidence, and creativity.

  • Thirukkural Isai Potti (Music Form): Sing a Kural chosen from a set of 100.
  • Thirukkural Oviyam Potti (Art Form): Draw a visual interpretation of a Kural from the same list.
  • Thirukkural Oduthal Potti (Recitation Form): Recite a Kural from the given set.
