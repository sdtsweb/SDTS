San Diego Tamil Sangam

San Diego Tamil Sangam (SDTS) is the cultural and social hub for Tamilians in the Greater San Diego area, fostering Tamil heritage and education.

  • Promotes Tamil cultural activities and festivals like Pongal, Tamil New Year, and Deepavali.
  • Supports Tamil language education through the Bharathiyar Tamil School.
  • Hosts annual events such as the Open House and Summer Picnic.
  • Led by an elected Board of Directors and Executive Committee.

Mission Statement

San Diego Tamil Sangam (SDTS) is dedicated to uniting and enriching the Tamil community in the Greater San Diego region by fostering the growth and preservation of Tamil culture, language, and heritage. We strive to provide a vibrant platform for cultural exchange, education, and celebration, ensuring that the rich traditions of Tamil Nadu are cherished and passed on to future generations.

Vision Statement

To be the cornerstone of Tamil cultural and social life in San Diego, inspiring and empowering our community to embrace and celebrate their heritage. We envision a thriving community where Tamil art, literature, language, and traditions flourish, fostering a sense of pride and unity among Tamilians of all ages. Through education, cultural programs, and community engagement, we aim to create an inclusive and dynamic environment that honors our past while building a vibrant future.

Bharathiyar Tamil School (BTS)

At Bharathiyar Tamil School, our goal is to introduce children from the age of three years and up to Tamil language and culture. Classes are taught in a fun, engaging, stress-free environment, with an emphasis on conversation skills, reading, and writing. The school follows the comprehensive, structured syllabus developed by the American Tamil Academy. The school also creates opportunities for children to participate in cultural activities such as plays, song and dance performances, display their budding skills on the stage, and interact with speakers who are thought leaders in the community and learn from them.

  • Completely volunteer-run program, dependent on diligent volunteering by parents & other interested volunteers.
  • Classes are held on Fridays between 6:30 p.m. & 8:30 p.m. at Shoal Creek Elementary School in Carmel Mountain Ranch.
  • BTS calendar year follows the Poway School District's regular school calendar.

Tamil Library

Our organization's Tamil library is operating at Bharathiyar Tamil School. It functions every Friday during school hours from 6:45 PM to 8:30 PM. Our library has justice stories for children and books for all age groups. We humbly request all members of the organization to utilize the library.
